FAQ

My package arrived damaged.

We apologize on behalf of the quality of your order upon delivery. Robostore offers refunds on quality issues, poor customer service and delivery issues only. You may contact thebeermen@mail.sfsu.edu to speak with a representative or refund processing.


I want a refund on my purchase.

Robostore offers refunds on quality issues, poor customer service and delivery issues only. You may contact thebeermen@mail.sfsu.edu for more information or refund processing.


When can I expect my package?

Robostore commits to delivering all merchandise within a timely manner. Packages delivered in the US should expect shipping within 1-3 business days. Packages shipped internationally will arrive 2-3 weeks from order date.


What delivery carrier does Robostore ship through?

Robostore commits to delivering all merchandise within a timely manner. Packages delivered in the US should expect shipping within 1-3 business days. Our primary delivery courier is Fedex. You may check the status of your order with your provided order number here.


Where can I find product specifications?

Robostore is a verified seller of robotics equipment, and merchandise. We understand specifications need to be listed to suit the needs of customers. Before ordering any product please read the specifications listed in the section below the product page to ensure parts fit your needs. We do not provide refunds for wrong sized or incompatible equipment. Under certain circumstances we may offer a substitution of parts if the required part is of equal or lower price, otherwise you will need to pay the equivalent prices for an upgrade or different model.


I can't find the part I am looking for.

Robostore provides a field to filter the product page and the homepage to help you find any product provided by Vendors held on the Robostore Site. We also include a search bar at the top center of our Site to search the name of any product listed in our store. If a product you are looking for is not found in any of these fields, we may not carry your specific item yet, and requests can be submitted through our "About" page.


Canceling an order.

You can cancel Product orders that haven't yet entered the shipping process by logging into your account, contacting Robostore as soon as possible through the Robostore support email. We will verify the status of your order and validate if it is cancellable and subject to any fees. Vendors put a lot of effort into delivering purchased Products rapidly. If an order has already shipped when you contact Robostore, the Product may then be returned subject to the terms in the Returns section. Products identified as Special Products may not be canceled if a purchase has already been processed specifically for you with the manufacturer. Restocking fees up to 20% may apply for large quantity order cancellations. Shipping charges are non-refundable. Robostore reserves the right to refuse or cancel any order in cases where an account has an outstanding balance.
